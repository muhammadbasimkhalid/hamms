Projects

Nakkai Clothing

Nakkai Clothing entrusted me with the task of developing a comprehensive online clothing store that would cater to a global audience while reflecting the diverse cultures and preferences of each region. The client’s vision was to create not just another e-commerce site but a platform that would establish Nakkai as a global brand capable of resonating with customers across different countries. To achieve this, I designed and developed a fully functional e-commerce platform that offered a wide range of clothing options for all ages and demographics. The store was meticulously organized to cater to men, women, and children, ensuring that every customer segment could easily find products suited to their style and needs. One of the standout features of the platform was the integration of a global map in the hero section. This innovative feature allowed users to select a country from the map, which then redirected them to a localized version of the Nakkai store, tailored specifically to that region’s cultural preferences and trends. Each localized store featured products, offers, and content relevant to the selected country, providing a seamless and personalized shopping experience for users around the world. In addition to the global map, the platform included advanced search functionality, multiple payment gateways, and responsive design, ensuring accessibility and ease of use on all devices. The site also featured dynamic content management, allowing Nakkai to update products and promotions efficiently. The client was extremely pleased with the final product, particularly the creative approach to global branding and the attention to detail in catering to different markets. This project successfully positioned Nakkai Clothing as a truly international brand, driving significant traffic and engagement from customers across various regions. This experience demonstrated my ability to lead complex projects, implement innovative solutions, and deliver results that align with client goals while meeting the demands of a global market.

Mess-India

As a Project Manager at Astral Developers, I led the development of "Mess India," an e-commerce platform designed for an Indian client who specialized in delivering meals to hostel students based on monthly subscriptions. The core challenge of this project lay in understanding and implementing the client's complex business model into a functional and user-friendly database system. The website needed to allow students to choose their meal packages and check the menu based on three primary budget categories: low, mid, and high. Within each budget category, meals were further divided by day (Monday to Sunday) and by time (morning, afternoon, evening). Each meal time offered two distinct dishes, which the client could update in real-time through a custom-built dashboard. To accommodate the client's needs, we developed a sophisticated backend system that allowed for easy management of meal options and user subscriptions. The dashboard provided the client with tools to monitor the number of users, update the food items according to the defined hierarchy, and ensure that the website met the operational demands of their business. The "Mess India" project showcases my ability to handle intricate database-driven web projects and deliver a tailored solution that aligns perfectly with the client's business objectives.
